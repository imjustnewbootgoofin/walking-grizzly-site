About the firm

Committed to sovereignty, community, and practical pathways

Walking Grizzly Bear and Consulting LLC is built around a simple idea. Listening closely, honoring Tribal sovereignty, and using clear, scenario based examples can help leaders and teams see their options more clearly.

Each engagement is designed to support the goals of the Tribe or community first. The firm does not direct decisions. Instead it offers illustrative models, planning frameworks, and general consulting insights so that leaders can make informed choices that align with their values.

All services are for educational and planning purposes only. The firm does not provide legal, tax, or regulated financial advice, and does not act as a representative or fiduciary.

What you can expect when we work together

  • Thoughtful preparation focused on the history, context, and priorities of your Tribe or community.
  • Meetings structured around respectful listening, clear questions, and scenario based exploration.
  • Written summaries that capture key options, example frameworks, and next step ideas in plain language.
  • Administrative guidance for coordinating partners, aligning programs, and organizing information.
  • Careful boundaries regarding what the firm can and cannot provide, including explicit limits on regulated advice.
Consulting focus areas

Scenario based consulting for programs and partnerships

Services are designed as general consulting support. They focus on illustrative models, planning options, and administrative guidance, rather than legal or tax opinions. Each engagement is shaped by the specific needs of your Tribe or organization.

Tribal consultation

Tribal and community consultation support

Support for meetings, listening sessions, and collaborative spaces where Tribes and partners come together to discuss programs, funding, and long term priorities. The focus is on preparation, facilitation support, and clear follow up notes that honor what was shared.

Workforce development

Workforce and program pathways

Scenario based exploration for youth and adult programs, training pathways, and community based workforce initiatives. Sessions can walk through example program structures, participant journeys, and options for aligning partners around shared outcomes.

Strategic planning

Strategic and program planning sessions

Collaborative planning support that uses visual models and scenario based examples to help teams think through near term actions and longer term directions. Sessions can include roadmaps, timelines, and options for phasing new efforts.

Grant & programs

Grant and program development consulting

General consulting insights for shaping program concepts, work plans, and narrative framing. Examples and templates can be used to think through roles, timelines, and community impact. All content is educational. Funding decisions remain with funders, and no guarantees are made or implied.
